# Build Your Own Annuity 💰📊

**An Excel-driven system for generating retirement income—without locking your money into an insurance annuity.**

---

## 🚀 Overview

This project shows you how to create a **monthly income portfolio** using ETFs and bonds that behaves like an annuity—but keeps your capital liquid and under your control.

Instead of handing over your money to an insurance company, you can:

* Generate consistent monthly income
* Maintain full access to your portfolio
* Adjust allocations anytime
* Potentially grow income over time

---

## 📊 What This Model Does

This Excel model helps you:

* Estimate **monthly income** from a $300,000 portfolio
* Adjust allocations across income, dividend, and bond ETFs
* Test different yield assumptions
* Compare income strategies

---

## 🧠 Strategy Breakdown

The model uses a **3-bucket income approach**:

### 💰 Income Engine (High Yield)

* Covered call ETFs for monthly income
* Higher yield, lower growth

### 📈 Dividend Growth (Inflation Protection)

* Dividend ETFs for rising income over time
* Lower yield, higher long-term growth

### 🛡️ Stability Layer (Risk Control)

* Bond ETFs to reduce volatility
* Provides consistency and balance

## ⚙️ How to Use

1. Open the Excel file
2. Go to the **Inputs** tab
3. Adjust:

   * Total investment
   * Allocation percentages
   * Yield assumptions
4. View results in the **Income** tab

👉 Your **monthly income updates automatically**
